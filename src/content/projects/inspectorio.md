> Any purchase orders from a brand need to go through a data integration channel to be visible on Inspectorio Sight.

In a defined schedule, inspections will be conducted by inspectors in the field
to ensure the products are being produced with the right design, quality and
packaging. An inspection can comprise multiple purchase orders if they have
similar characteristics, others need to be separate.

![alt text](/assets/Inspectorio-inspection-case.png)

The completed inspection will come with an inspection report. The Sight
platform takes care of all the calculation and format tasks, allowing inspectors
to focus on their areas of expertise. No more paperwork and delay time!

![alt text](/assets/Inspectorio-inspection-report.png)

Following up with the inspection report, a correction and presentation action
plan could be formed and executed by the manufacturers. This process is fully
supported by Sight with many automation and suggestions, improving the
performance and collaboration effectiveness between parties.

![alt text](/assets/Inspectorio-defect-report.png)

During my 2.5 years on the Sight platform, I gained experience in all aspects of
the product including user roles, notifications, purchase orders, inspections,
and reports.
