> Designed and delivered an end-to-end inspection platform that streamlined manual workflows, enabled real-time reporting, and improved operational efficiency at scale

#### Problem

Quality Control and Compliance processes were extremely manual and scattered, leading to uninformed decision making:
- Manual, paper-based, and inconsistent factory inspection report data from siloed systems
- Poor visibility and proof over the integrity and accuracy of factory inspections
- Scattered and incomplete data regarding quality control of purchase orders
- Inefficient inspector allocation, resulting in delayed manufacturing and shipment or even failed orders 

---

#### Solution

Designed and implemented an end-to-end inspection workflow from purchase order to shipment:
- Digitalised the entire factory inspection process, consisting of a mobile application for inspectors and a robust web portal for inspection managers
- Designed real-time inspection tracking to enable managers to identify issues immediately
- Built a unified, consistent, and complete data flow covering all aspect of quality control for purchase orders
- Implemented a robust and intelligent inspector allocation system which maximises inspection performance, integrity, and timing

---

#### Impact

- 🚀 Reduced manual inspection monitoring and scheduling effort by ~70% through workflow digitalisation and automation  
- ⚙️ Eliminated data inconsistencies by standardising inspection data models and integrating siloed systems  
- 👁️ Enabled real-time inspection visibility, allowing managers to identify and resolve issues proactively  
- 📈 Accelerated time-to-market by ~15% by improving inspection turnaround and coordination across vendors  
- 🛠️ Reduced final product defect rate by ~20% through improved inspection accuracy and early issue detection  

---

#### Scale

- 50+ vendors onboarded in the pilot program
- Multiple global retailers (Target, Kohl’s, Crocs)  
- Cross-region inspection operations 

---

#### Key Contributions

- **End-to-End Workflow Design**: Designed the full inspection lifecycle from purchase order to shipment, aligning business and system processes  
- **Stakeholder Alignment**: Collaborated with global retailers and vendors to standardise inspection processes and data definitions  
- **Product & Feature Definition**: Defined core features including inspection tracking, reporting, and integrity validation  
- **Data Model Design**: Structured standardised inspection data models to support scalability and API integrations  
- **Implementation Leadership**: Led delivery during pilot phase and supported rollout to 50+ vendors
